The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices hearty wheat sandwich bread
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons jelly or jam

Directions

  1. Spread 1 tablespoons peanut butter onto 1 slice of bread.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon jelly onto another slice of bread.
  3. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  4. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich into half.
